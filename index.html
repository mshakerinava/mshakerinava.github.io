CV


I am a 4th year PhD candidate at McGill University and Mila, supervised by Prof. Siamak Ravanbakhsh. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, optimization, and geometry, with a focus on theoretical foundations and practical applications of symmetry in learning algorithms. Prior to McGill, I received a Bachelor's degree in computer engineering from Sharif University of Technology in Iran. If you'd like to discuss research, potential collaboration, or just connect, feel free to email me.

Research Interests

  • Design and analysis of machine learning algorithms
  • Symmetry and geometry in machine learning and optimization
  • Statistical learning theory and theoretical foundations
  • Utility theory and reinforcement learning
